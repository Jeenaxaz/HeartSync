### Project Description
HeartSync is a couples' emotional sync web app where the girl can track her menstrual cycle, moods, and stress levels — and the boy can view her status, send love messages, get smart message suggestions, and be reminded of important dates. It bridges emotional understanding between partners through a simple shared dashboard.

### The Problem Statement
Partners often struggle to understand each other's emotional and physical states, especially around menstrual cycles or high-stress periods. Forgetting important dates or saying the wrong thing at the wrong time causes unnecessary friction in relationships.

### The Solution
HeartSync connects couples through a private, match-code–based pairing system. The girl logs her daily mood, stress, and cycle data; the boy gets a real-time view of her status alongside smart message suggestions and timely reminders for upcoming special dates — all in one lightweight, browser-based app.

## Features

- 💞 **Secure Couple Pairing** — Girl signs up and gets a unique match code; boy logs in by entering her code to link their accounts
- 🌸 **Girl Dashboard** — Track period start date & cycle length, log mood, family/academic/health stress sliders, and cravings/needs
- 💙 **Boy Dashboard** — View partner's live status, update own mood, send love messages, and get context-aware message suggestions
- 📅 **Important Dates Tracker** — Add birthdays, anniversaries, and special days with emoji labels, countdown timers, and urgent reminders (within 5 days)
